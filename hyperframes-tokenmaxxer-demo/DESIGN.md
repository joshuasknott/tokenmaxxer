# TokenMaxxer Cinematic Demo

## Style Prompt

Clean, cinematic product demo for an open-source desktop app. The visual language is dark, precise, and local-first: a real TokenMaxxer UI shot floats on a deep blue-black canvas with restrained teal/blue light, soft depth, and slow camera movement. No invented dashboards, no fake panels, no generic SaaS mockups.

## Colors

- Canvas: `#071016`
- Elevated UI: `#0b1117`
- Foreground: `#f3f7fb`
- Muted text: `#9aa8b7`
- TokenMaxxer accent: `#60a5fa`
- Usage accent: `#22c5b7`

## Typography

- Primary: system UI stack for exact consistency with the TokenMaxxer app.
- Data and timing: tabular numbers via `font-variant-numeric: tabular-nums`.

## What NOT to Do

- Do not invent product screens or redraw UI with generic cards.
- Do not use purple-blue marketing gradients or decorative blobs.
- Do not crowd the frame with explanatory copy.
- Do not cut off the product UI or shrink it until labels become illegible.
